PART I

Item 1. Business

Overview

Amazon.com, Inc. was incorporated in 1994 in the state of Delaware. Amazon is guided by four
principles: customer obsession, passion for invention, commitment to operational excellence, and
long-term thinking. Amazon strives to be Earth's most customer-centric company, Earth's best
employer, and Earth's safest place to work.

The Company's principal executive offices are located at 410 Terry Avenue North, Seattle,
Washington 98109-5210. The Company's telephone number is (206) 266-1000. The Company's website
address is www.amazon.com. The Company's common stock is listed on the Nasdaq Global Select Market
under the symbol AMZN.

Amazon's fiscal year ends on December 31.


Products and Services

North America Segment
The North America segment includes retail sales of consumer products and subscriptions through
North America-focused websites such as www.amazon.com and www.amazon.ca. It also includes sales
through Amazon's physical stores and advertising services. In fiscal 2022, North America segment
net sales were $315,880 million. North America segment operating income was $3,531 million
in fiscal 2022.

International Segment
The International segment includes retail sales of consumer products and subscriptions through
internationally focused websites. In fiscal 2022, International segment net sales were
$118,007 million. International operating loss was $7,746 million in fiscal 2022, primarily
due to macroeconomic pressures including the impact of foreign exchange rates.

Amazon Web Services (AWS)
AWS serves millions of active customers in more than 240 countries and territories. AWS offers
a broad set of cloud computing services including compute, storage, database, analytics,
networking, machine learning and AI, developer tools, IoT, and security services. In fiscal 2022,
AWS net sales were $80,096 million, an increase of $21,358 million or 29% from fiscal 2021.
AWS operating income was $22,841 million in fiscal 2022.


Human Capital

Amazon employed approximately 1,541,000 full-time and part-time employees as of December 31,
2022. This represents a decrease of approximately 99,000 employees from December 31, 2021, when
the Company had approximately 1,622,000 employees.

Item 2. Properties

Amazon's principal executive offices are located in Seattle, Washington. Amazon operates a
globally distributed network of fulfillment centers, delivery stations, data centers, and
corporate offices. The Company's fulfillment and data center network spans millions of square
feet across North America, Europe, Asia, and other regions.

Total net sales increased $44,161 million or 9% from fiscal 2021 to fiscal 2022. Amazon reported
a net loss of $2,722 million in fiscal 2022, compared to net income of $33,364 million in fiscal
2021, primarily due to a pre-tax valuation loss of $12,700 million included in non-operating
expense from Amazon's common stock investment in Rivian Automotive, Inc.

AWS remains Amazon's most profitable segment, contributing $22,841 million in operating income,
offsetting losses in the North America and International segments during fiscal 2022.

Amazon's customers include consumers, sellers, developers, enterprises, and content creators
worldwide. Prime membership provides customers with access to free shipping, streaming video,
streaming music, and other benefits.
