PART I

Item 1. Business

Overview

Alphabet Inc. is a holding company whose principal subsidiary is Google LLC. Alphabet was created
as part of a corporate restructuring of Google in 2015. Alphabet's businesses span technology
products and services including online advertising, cloud computing, consumer electronics, and
other services through subsidiaries including Google, Waymo, DeepMind, and Verily.

The Company was incorporated in Delaware. Alphabet's principal executive offices are located at
1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, California 94043. The Company's telephone number is
(650) 253-0000. The Company's website address is www.abc.xyz. Alphabet's Class A common stock
and Class C capital stock are listed on the Nasdaq Global Select Market under the symbols GOOGL
and GOOG, respectively.

Alphabet's fiscal year ends on December 31.


Products and Services

Google Services
Google Services includes Google's advertising products (Google Search, YouTube ads, Google
Network), Google's subscription and device products (Google Play, Google One, Pixel phones,
Nest), and other Google services. In fiscal 2022, Google Services revenue was $253,528 million.

Google Advertising Revenue (included within Google Services):
  - Google Search and other:     $162,450 million
  - YouTube ads:                  $29,243 million
  - Google Network:               $32,780 million

Google Cloud
Google Cloud includes Google's infrastructure and data analytics platforms, collaboration tools,
and other services for enterprise customers. In fiscal 2022, Google Cloud revenue was
$26,280 million, an increase of $6,107 million or 30% from fiscal 2021.

Other Bets
Other Bets includes early-stage technology businesses such as Waymo (autonomous vehicles),
Verily (life sciences), and other ventures. In fiscal 2022, Other Bets revenue was
approximately $1,068 million.


Human Capital

As of December 31, 2022, Alphabet had 190,234 full-time employees. During 2022, Alphabet
significantly expanded its workforce. However, in January 2023, the Company announced a
reduction in force of approximately 12,000 roles.

Item 2. Properties

Alphabet's principal offices are located in Mountain View, California. The Company's properties
include approximately 25.9 million square feet of office space globally.

Total revenues decreased from fiscal 2021 to fiscal 2022, reflecting a challenging macroeconomic
environment that led to reduced advertiser spending, particularly in the second half of 2022.
YouTube ad revenues declined year-over-year for the first time since Alphabet began disclosing
that metric separately.

Google Cloud achieved its first full-year operating profit contribution in fiscal 2022. Alphabet's
target customers include online advertisers, enterprise cloud customers, and consumers worldwide.
