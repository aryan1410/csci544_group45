PART I

Item 1. Business

Overview

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables,
and accessories worldwide. The Company also sells a variety of related services. The Company's fiscal
year is the 52 or 53-week period that ends on the last Saturday of September. The Company's fiscal
year 2023 ended on September 30, 2023 and spanned 53 weeks.

The Company was incorporated in California on January 3, 1977. The Company's principal executive
offices are located at One Apple Park Way, Cupertino, California 95014. The Company's telephone
number is (408) 996-1010. The Company's website address is www.apple.com. The Company's common
stock is listed on the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC under the symbol AAPL.

The Company's principal products and services are:
  - iPhone: a line of smartphones
  - Mac: a line of personal computers
  - iPad: a line of multi-purpose tablets
  - Wearables, Home and Accessories: includes AirPods, Apple TV, Apple Watch, Beats products, and HomePod
  - Services: includes advertising, AppleCare, cloud services, digital content, payment services, and licensing


Products and Services

iPhone
iPhone is the Company's line of smartphones based on its iOS operating system. iPhone includes
accessories and peripherals compatible with iPhone. In fiscal 2023, iPhone net sales were
$200,583 million, representing approximately 52% of the Company's total net sales.

Mac
Mac is the Company's line of personal computers based on its macOS operating system. Mac includes
accessories and displays compatible with Mac. In fiscal 2023, Mac net sales were $29,357 million.

iPad
iPad is the Company's line of multi-purpose tablets based on its iPadOS operating system. iPad
includes accessories compatible with iPad. In fiscal 2023, iPad net sales were $28,300 million.

Wearables, Home and Accessories
Wearables, Home and Accessories includes products such as AirPods, Apple TV, Apple Watch, Beats
products, and HomePod. AirPods are the Company's wireless headphones. Apple Watch is the
Company's line of smartwatches based on its watchOS operating system. Apple TV is the Company's
digital media player based on its tvOS operating system. In fiscal 2023, Wearables, Home and
Accessories net sales were $39,845 million.

Services
Services includes advertising, AppleCare, cloud services, digital content, payment services, and
licensing. The Company's Services offerings include:
  - Advertising: third-party licensing arrangements and the Company's own advertising platforms
  - AppleCare: technical support and hardware repair services
  - Cloud services: iCloud storage and related services
  - Digital content: the App Store, Apple Arcade, Apple Books, Apple Fitness+, Apple Music, Apple
    News+, Apple One, and Apple TV+
  - Payment services: Apple Card, Apple Cash, and Apple Pay
  - Licensing: intellectual property licensing arrangements

In fiscal 2023, Services net sales were $85,200 million, an increase of $7,071 million or 9% from
fiscal 2022.


Distribution and Sales

The Company distributes its products and resells third-party products in most of its major markets
directly to consumers, small and mid-sized businesses, and education, enterprise, and government
customers through its retail and online stores and its direct sales force. The Company also utilizes
a variety of indirect distribution channels, such as third-party cellular network carriers,
wholesalers, retailers, and value-added resellers.

In fiscal 2023, the Company's net sales through its direct and indirect distribution channels
accounted for 37% and 63%, respectively, of total net sales.

As of September 30, 2023, the Company operated 518 retail stores in 25 countries. The Company's
retail stores offer a range of the Company's products and services, as well as the products of
select third-party suppliers.


Human Capital

As of September 30, 2023, the Company had approximately 161,000 full-time equivalent employees.
This compares to approximately 164,000 full-time equivalent employees as of September 24, 2022,
a decrease of approximately 3,000 employees.

The Company believes its success depends substantially on its ability to attract, develop, and
retain key personnel. The Company is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace.


Competition

The markets for the Company's products and services are highly competitive and are characterized by
aggressive price competition, frequent product introductions, and rapid technological changes. The
Company's competitors who sell mobile communications and media devices and personal computers have
significantly broader product lines and greater financial resources than the Company. The Company
focuses on the innovation, quality, design, and customer experience of its products and services.


Item 1A. Risk Factors

The following risk factors could materially adversely affect the Company's business, reputation,
results of operations, financial condition, and stock price, and could cause actual results to
differ materially from those projected in any forward-looking statements. The Company's business,
financial condition, and operating results can be affected by a number of factors, whether
currently known or unknown, including but not limited to the following:

  - Dependence on the performance of iPhone: The Company has historically derived the majority
    of its net sales from its iPhone line of products.
  - Global economic conditions and foreign currency exchange rate fluctuations: The Company has
    substantial international operations and a significant portion of its revenue is generated
    outside the United States.
  - Competition: The markets for the Company's products and services are highly competitive.
  - Supply chain risks: The Company relies on single-source suppliers for certain components.
  - Regulatory risks: The Company is subject to complex and changing laws and regulations
    worldwide, including data privacy, antitrust, and tax matters.
  - Intellectual property risks: The Company regularly files patent applications to protect
    innovations developed by the Company.

Item 2. Properties

The Company's headquarters are located in Cupertino, California. As of September 30, 2023, the
Company owned or leased facilities and land for corporate functions, R&D, data centers, retail
and other purposes. As of September 30, 2023, the Company operated 518 retail stores in 25
countries totaling approximately 5.6 million square feet. The Company owns its principal
headquarters facilities in Cupertino, California, encompassing approximately 6.1 million square
feet.


Additional Notes

The Company's fiscal year 2022 total net sales of $394,328 million represent a decline to
$383,285 million in fiscal year 2023, a decrease of $11,043 million or 2.8%.

Mac net sales decreased $10,820 million or 27% from fiscal 2022 to fiscal 2023, primarily due to
the challenging macroeconomic environment and a difficult year-over-year comparison.

Services net sales increased $7,071 million or 9% from fiscal 2022 to fiscal 2023, driven by
growth across all Services categories.

iPhone net sales decreased $4,906 million or 2% from fiscal 2022 to fiscal 2023.

Wearables, Home and Accessories net sales decreased $1,396 million or 3% from fiscal 2022 to
fiscal 2023.

iPad net sales decreased $992 million or 3% from fiscal 2022 to fiscal 2023.

The Company's target markets include consumers, small and mid-sized businesses, and education,
enterprise, and government customers.

Apple's iPhone uses the iOS operating system. Apple's Mac computers use the macOS operating
system. Apple's iPad uses the iPadOS operating system. Apple's Apple Watch uses the watchOS
operating system. Apple's Apple TV uses the tvOS operating system.
