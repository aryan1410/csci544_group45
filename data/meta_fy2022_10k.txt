PART I

Item 1. Business

Overview

Meta Platforms, Inc. builds technologies that help people connect, find communities, and grow
businesses. Meta's family of apps includes Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and WhatsApp. The
Company also develops the Meta Quest virtual reality headset and related hardware. Meta was
incorporated in Delaware in 2004 and changed its name from Facebook, Inc. to Meta Platforms, Inc.
in October 2021.

The Company's principal executive offices are located at 1601 Willow Road, Menlo Park, California
94025. The Company's telephone number is (650) 543-4800. The Company's website address is
www.meta.com. The Company's Class A common stock is listed on the Nasdaq Global Select Market
under the symbol META.

Meta's fiscal year ends on December 31.


Products and Services

Family of Apps (FoA)
The Family of Apps segment includes Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and other services.
Revenue in this segment is primarily generated from advertising. In fiscal 2022, Family of Apps
revenue was $114,450 million. Family of Apps operating income was $42,661 million in fiscal 2022.

  Facebook: a social networking platform connecting billions of users worldwide
  Instagram: a photo and video sharing platform
  Messenger: a messaging application
  WhatsApp: an end-to-end encrypted messaging service with over 2 billion users

Reality Labs (RL)
The Reality Labs segment includes augmented and virtual reality related consumer hardware,
software, and content. Products include the Meta Quest virtual reality headset lineup. In fiscal
2022, Reality Labs revenue was $2,159 million. Reality Labs operating loss was $13,717 million
in fiscal 2022.

Key metrics (as of December 31, 2022):
  - Facebook Daily Active Users (DAUs): 2.00 billion
  - Facebook Monthly Active Users (MAUs): 2.96 billion
  - Family Daily Active People (DAP): 2.96 billion
  - Family Monthly Active People (MAP): 3.74 billion


Human Capital

As of December 31, 2022, Meta had approximately 86,482 full-time employees, a significant
reduction from 87,314 employees at the end of 2021. In November 2022, Meta announced a
restructuring that included a reduction in workforce of approximately 11,000 employees.

Item 2. Properties

Meta's principal offices are located in Menlo Park, California. The Company also maintains offices
in major cities around the world. Meta owns and leases approximately 22.9 million square feet of
building space globally.

Total revenue decreased from $117,929 million in fiscal 2021 to $116,609 million in fiscal 2022,
a decrease of $1,320 million or 1%, primarily due to a decline in advertising revenue caused by
macroeconomic pressures and privacy-related changes to mobile platforms.

The Reality Labs segment continues to operate at a significant loss as the Company invests in
building the metaverse. Meta's target customers include advertisers, developers, businesses, and
consumers worldwide.
