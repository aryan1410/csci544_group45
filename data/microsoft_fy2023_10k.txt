PART I

Item 1. Business

Overview

Microsoft Corporation develops and supports software, services, devices, and solutions worldwide.
The Company was founded in 1975. Microsoft's mission is to empower every person and every
organization on the planet to achieve more. The Company's fiscal year ends on June 30.

The Company is incorporated in the state of Washington. The Company's principal executive offices
are located at One Microsoft Way, Redmond, Washington 98052-6399. The Company's telephone number
is (425) 882-8080. The Company's website address is www.microsoft.com. The Company's common stock
is listed on the Nasdaq Global Select Market under the symbol MSFT.


Products and Services

Productivity and Business Processes
This segment includes products and services in the portfolio of Office, Exchange, SharePoint,
Microsoft Teams, Office 365 Security and Compliance, and Microsoft Viva (Productivity and
Business Processes). The segment also includes LinkedIn and Dynamics business solutions. In fiscal
2023, Productivity and Business Processes revenue was $69,274 million.

Intelligent Cloud
This segment includes server products and cloud services, including Microsoft Azure, SQL Server,
Windows Server, Visual Studio, System Center, and GitHub. In fiscal 2023, Intelligent Cloud revenue
was $87,907 million. Azure and other cloud services revenue grew 29% year-over-year in fiscal 2023.

More Personal Computing
This segment includes Windows, devices (including Surface and PC accessories), gaming (including
Xbox hardware, Xbox content and services, and first-party content), and search and news advertising.
In fiscal 2023, More Personal Computing revenue was $54,734 million.


Human Capital

As of June 30, 2023, Microsoft employed approximately 221,000 full-time employees, with
approximately 122,000 employees in the United States and approximately 99,000 employees
internationally.


Distribution

Microsoft distributes its products and services through the following channels: OEM, direct, and
third-party resellers. Cloud services are available directly through Microsoft or through
partner resellers.

Item 2. Properties

Microsoft's principal executive offices are located in Redmond, Washington. The Company owns
approximately 15 million square feet of building space worldwide and leases approximately
8 million square feet of building space globally, primarily in the United States.

The Company's fiscal year 2023 ended on June 30, 2023. Total revenue increased $13,645 million
or approximately 7% from fiscal 2022 to fiscal 2023.

Microsoft's target customers include enterprise, commercial, government, educational, and consumer
markets worldwide.

The Company's cloud-first strategy is reflected in the strong growth of Intelligent Cloud, which
at $87,907 million represents the Company's largest revenue segment in fiscal 2023.
